GeoAI Research Scientist
------------------------

I am a junior research scientist specializing in the dynamic intersection of geospatial artificial intelligence (GeoAI) and crop niche change monitoring. Currently pursuing a PhD in Geography at Michigan State University, I am deeply immersed in exploring innovative methodologies to address pressing food security challenges. Driven by a profound dedication to leveraging technology for the betterment of both local and global communities, my work exemplifies the fusion of academic rigor with hands-on community engagement.

*   🌍  I'm based in Lansing, Michigan.

*   🤝  I'm open to collaborating on Google Collab, Google Earth Engine...
